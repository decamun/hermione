Hey there Everyone!

Hermione is a simple web app that was created to let people ask questions in real time during a lecture or presentation, without interrupting the flow of things. And yes, of course it’s named after our favorite know-it-all.

During Hermione’s premier at the PennApps demo we’re inviting YOU to ask us anything - anything at all - on this page. (Just don’t make it weird.) Hopefully we will answer you onstage. And even if we don't, we hope you have fun!

Happy demo-ing!

-Hermione Team

PS.

The three buttons let you vote on other people’s questions. A heart means you too would like to know the answer of this question. An ‘x’ means the opposite. A check mark means you think everyone has already heard the answer to this question. Have fun!
